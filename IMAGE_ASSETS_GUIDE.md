## Image Specifications

### Product Images
- Format: PNG with transparent background
- Size: 400x400px minimum
- Aspect Ratio: 1:1 (square)

### Section Images
- Format: JPG
- Quality: High (80-90%)
- Hero: 1920x1080px
- Experience: 800x600px
- Materials: 600x800px (large), 600x400px (small)

### Testimonial Images
- Format: JPG
- Size: 400x400px
- Aspect Ratio: 1:1 (square)
- Style: Professional headshots or lifestyle photos

## Temporary Solution

For development purposes, you can:
1. Use placeholder images from services like:
   - https://unsplash.com (furniture, interior design)
   - https://pexels.com (free stock photos)
   - https://placehold.co (placeholder generator)

2. Or use CSS gradients as temporary placeholders until real images are ready.
